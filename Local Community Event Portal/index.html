Community Bulletin

Local Park Renovation Complete

The renovation of Central Park has been completed ahead of schedule. The park now features new playground equipment, improved walking paths, and additional seating areas for families to enjoy.

"We're thrilled to see this project come to fruition," said Parks Director Jane Smith. "The community's input was invaluable in creating a space that everyone can enjoy."

Library Extends Hours for Summer

The Main Street Library has announced extended hours for the summer months. Starting June 1st, the library will remain open until 8 PM on weekdays and will also be open on Sundays from 12 PM to 5 PM.

New Community Garden Initiative

The City Council has approved funding for three new community gardens to be established in different neighborhoods. Residents interested in participating can sign up for a plot starting next month.

Youth Summer Programs Registration Open

Registration is now open for the city's summer youth programs, including sports camps, art workshops, and STEM activities. Early registration discounts are available until May 31st.
