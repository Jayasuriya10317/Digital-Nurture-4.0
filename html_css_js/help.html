Help & FAQ

Getting Started

Welcome to the Local Community Event Portal help page. Here you'll find information about how to use our portal to register for events, find events near you, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Navigate to the main page and scroll down to the "Event Registration" section. Fill out the form with your details and select the event you wish to attend.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration by contacting us directly at info@communityevents.example with your name and the event details.

How do I find events near me?

Use the "Find Nearby Events" button in the location section. This will use your device's geolocation to find events in your area.

Are there any fees for using this portal?

No, the portal is free to use. However, some events may have registration fees which will be displayed when you select the event type.

How can I provide feedback about an event?

After attending an event, you can use the "Event Feedback" section on the main page to share your thoughts and experiences.
